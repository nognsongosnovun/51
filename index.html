Chameleons

  1. Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  2. Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons.
  3. Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Pigeons

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  2. Pigeons are renowed for their outsanding navigational abilities.
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Camels

  1. Camels can go for weeks without drinking water.
  2. Camels store fat in parts of their bodies known as humps.
  3. Camel milk is among the healthiest milk in the world.

Pandas

  1. Pandas have great camouflage for their environment.
  2. Pandas spend most of their day eating.
  3. Pandas do occasionally eat something other than bamboo, which is critical in their diet.
